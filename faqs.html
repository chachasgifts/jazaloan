Loans & Tax Help

We support reducing balance, flat rate, SACCO loans, HELB, and mobile loans such as M-Shwari, Fuliza, and KCB M-Pesa.

Reducing balance calculates interest on the remaining loan balance, while flat rate applies interest on the original amount. Reducing balance is generally cheaper long-term.

PAYE is based on income bands set by KRA, plus deductions for NSSF, SHIF, and housing levy. Our calculator applies all these rules automatically.
