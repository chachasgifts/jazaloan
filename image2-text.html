How to Use Image to Text Converter Online?

Follow these three simple steps to extract the text from the picture.

  1. Upload Images: Upload images by drag & drop, selecting from your computer, or pasting them directly.
  2. Start Conversion: Click “Convert”. Our image to text converter will quickly process your images and provide the text in a digital format.
