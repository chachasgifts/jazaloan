How to Use the Kenya Loan Calculator – JazaLoan

  • Enter the loan amount in Kenyan Shillings (e.g., 50,000 Ksh).
  • Type in the annual interest rate given by your lender (e.g., 13%).
  • Enter the repayment period in months (e.g., 12 months).
  • Select the repayment type:
    • Fixed Principal – Same principal each month, interest decreases over time. Common with SACCO loans.
    • Equal Installments – Fixed total payment each month, including both principal and interest. Common for bank loans and hire purchase.
  • Choose the interest method:
    • Reducing Balance – Interest charged only on the remaining balance each month, leading to lower total interest.
    • Flat Rate – Interest charged on the original loan amount for the whole term.
  • Click Calculate to see your monthly payment, total repayment, and total interest payable.

Tip: Compare reducing balance and flat rate loans before borrowing — it could save you thousands.

A reducing balance loan calculator helps you estimate your monthly loan repayments in Kenya by applying interest only to the remaining loan balance. As the principal reduces each month, the interest charged also decreases—making it more affordable long-term.
